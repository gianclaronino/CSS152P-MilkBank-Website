Donation Guidelines

Donated milk will undergo screening, pasteurization, and quality testing before distribution. Donors must meet health eligibility criteria including serological testing. All data is handled in compliance with the Data Privacy Act of the Philippines (RA 10173).

Data Privacy Consent

Your personal and medical information will be collected, stored securely, and used solely for milk bank operations. You may request access or deletion of your records at any time.
