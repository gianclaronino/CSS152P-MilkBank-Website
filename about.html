Nourishing lives through safe donor milk.

What we do

A simple process that keeps donor milk safe and traceable.

Mission

Ensure that every premature or ill infant has access to safe human milk when a mother's own milk is unavailable.

Vision

Create a community where no infant is denied the nutritional and immunological benefits of human milk.

Standards

Screening, pasteurization, quality checks, and traceability all stay tightly controlled from intake to release.

Support

Donors, staff, and hospitals can move through the process without losing clarity or confidence.
