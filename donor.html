Donor Consent & Guidelines

Donation Guidelines & Safety

All donated human milk is subject to heat treatment (Holder Pasteurization) to eliminate potential pathogens. By proceeding, you acknowledge that your milk will be pooled with milk from other screened donors to ensure nutrient consistency.

Serological Screening

Donors must undergo mandatory blood testing for HIV-1/2, Hepatitis B, Hepatitis C, and Syphilis. Cost of these tests is fully covered by the Bangkal Milk Bank. Results are strictly confidential.

Data Privacy Statement

Your records (medical history, test results, donation volumes) are encrypted and stored in compliance with the Data Privacy Act (RA 10173). Access is limited to authorized medical staff for safety tracking only.

Usage of Donated Milk

Priority is given to hospitalized premature infants in neonatal intensive care units (NICU). Any surplus may be used for medical research under IRB-approved protocols unless you explicitly opt-out.
