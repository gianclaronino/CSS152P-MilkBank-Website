Our Process

How it works

1

Register Online

Fill out our secure online form to start your journey as a milk donor. It’s quick, easy, and the first step to making a difference.

2

Health Screening

Our team will guide you through a simple health screening process to ensure the safety of the milk for our recipients.

3

Donate Milk

Once approved, you can donate your milk at our facility or arrange for a pickup. Every drop counts!

4

Nourish a Baby

Your donation is carefully processed and given to premature and sick infants, giving them a fighting chance at a healthy life.
